## The Bound

The Bekenstein bound caps the entropy of a region by its boundary area rather
than its volume:

$$
S \leq \frac{A}{4 \, \ell_P^2}
$$

where $A$ is the area of the boundary and $\ell_P$ the Planck length. That the
bound scales with area, not volume, is the seed of the holographic principle.
